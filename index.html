Getting Started
Nancy is one of many friendly Computer Science students at the University of Pennsylvania.
Nancy's best work comes when she gets to combine what she knows about programming, design, and cognition together to make something super cool.
Setting Up
Nancy is easy to install! Ask her to design something, program something, or edit your paper, and she will jump at the chance to try something new. If neither of those are handy, all you have to do is entertain her with a horrendous pun or an interesting-looking fruit and she will pretty much follow you wherever.
